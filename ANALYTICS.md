# Analytics & KPI Spec

## 1. Overview
In V1, tracking should be minimal, respecting the privacy-first approach outlined in the PRD.

## 2. Core Metrics (If Analytics Implemented)
- **Pageviews:** To identify the most popular calculators.
- **Time on Page:** To gauge engagement with specific tools.
- **Bounce Rate:** To identify poorly performing entry points.

## 3. Privacy Considerations
- No personally identifiable information (PII) should be tracked.
- Do not log the specific data values users enter into the calculators. Track only that a calculation occurred.
