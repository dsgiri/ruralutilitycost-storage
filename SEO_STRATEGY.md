# SEO Strategy Document

## 1. Goal
Drive organic traffic from agricultural operators searching for specific calculation tools, capacity estimates, and compliance guidelines.

## 2. Keyword Themes by Page
- **Homepage:** "agricultural storage calculators", "grain bin capacity tools", "farm storage planning"
- **Grain Bin Capacity:** "grain bin capacity calculator", "how many bushels in a grain bin", "grain bin volume estimator"
- **Feed Storage Capacity:** "feed storage capacity calculator", "silo tonnage calculator", "dry feed volume limits"
- **Equipment Storage Planner:** "tractor storage square footage calculator", "farm equipment shed planner"
- **Tank Volume & EPA Auditor:** "agricultural liquid tank volume calculator", "farm tank EPA compliance screening"
- **Inventory Rotation Planner:** "agricultural inventory FIFO planner", "farm perishable inventory rotation"
- **Spoilage Risk Assessor:** "grain spoilage risk calculator", "feed moisture temperature risk"
- **Storage Cost Analysis:** "grain carry cost calculator", "agricultural storage overhead analysis"

## 3. On-Page SEO Requirements
- **Title Tags:** Format as `[Tool Name] | Rural Utility Cost Storage Hub`.
- **Meta Descriptions:** 150-160 characters describing the tool's utility and including the primary keyword.
- **H1 Tags:** Exactly one `H1` per page, clearly stating the tool's name.
- **Semantic Structure:** Use `H2` for sections like "How it Works" and "Definitions".
- **Internal Linking:** Ensure every tool links to at least two related tools to build topical clusters.

## 4. Technical SEO
- Clean, static URLs (e.g., `/tools/grain-bin-capacity`).
- Fast load times (Lighthouse performance > 90).
- Mobile-responsive design (passes Google Mobile-Friendly Test).
- No blocking of crawlers in `robots.txt` for public pages.
